Call For Proposals (CFP)

Submit a talk!

Why give a talk?

We'd love to have you talk here in Buffalo for our first Ruby conference. We'll be accepting talks through May 15th. Even if this would be for first presentation in front of an audience, we hope that you will apply to speak and share your story!

Talk Format

Talks should be about 30 minutes and there will be a short break in between talks. Talks will be recorded (given speaker authorization) and made freely available online when they are ready.

Giving a Successful Talk

You don't have to be the expert to have an interesting story to share. There are many techniques and ways to tell your story.

How We Pick

Speakers will be chosen using blind picking (hiding any information distinguishing the speaker) made up of a small committee.

The Reveal

We will contact all those applied. Please make sure to include a valid email so that we can get a hold of you with further details.

If You Have Questions

Email us at nickelcityruby@gmail.com or tweet at us.
