venue

We couldn't have picked a better place for learning: a library!

Downtown Buffalo is full of architectural gems, and our conference is set in the middle of it: The Buffalo & Erie County Public Library's Mason O. Dixon Auditorium. Right outside is Lafayette Square, which has long been a center for the city's civic and political activity.

travel

We want you not only come to the conference, but see some of what our region has to offer. There's plenty of attractions to check out in the area while you're in town. We're also organizing a rideshare from the airport to downtown to welcome our attendees to the region.

Need a hotel room? We'd suggest the Hyatt Regency, Comfort Suites, or the Hotel Lafayette. There's also some great places in local neighborhoods on AirBNB.
